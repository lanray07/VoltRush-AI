# App Store Listing Draft

## App Name

VoltRush AI

## Subtitle

Electrician learning simulator

## Description

Train your electrical knowledge through a game-first learning simulator built for apprentices, students, qualified electricians, and contractors.

VoltRush AI turns practical electrical concepts into interactive missions, fault diagnosis challenges, wiring puzzles, timed quizzes, AI mentor explanations, and career progression. Start as an apprentice, complete simulated jobs, earn XP and coins, unlock badges, and build toward contractor and company-owner status.

Practice safe diagnostic thinking with scenarios such as dead sockets, tripping breakers, lighting faults, overloaded panels, EV charger planning, solar inverter troubleshooting, and emergency callouts. The Wiring Lab lets you connect circuit nodes in drag-and-drop style puzzles, while Quiz Arena offers practice, timed, and boss battle modes across safety, tools, regulations, fault finding, calculations, EV chargers, solar, and inspection/testing.

VoltRush AI is an educational simulator only. It does not replace formal electrician training, licensing, certification, manufacturer instructions, local regulations, workplace procedures, or qualified professional guidance.
