# VoltRush AI Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 11, 2026

VoltRush AI is an educational electrical learning simulator. It is designed for learning and simulation only and does not replace formal electrician training, licensing, certification, local regulations, or qualified professional guidance.

## Data Collection

The current MVP of VoltRush AI does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data with the developer.

The app does not require an account, does not include third-party advertising, and does not use third-party analytics in this MVP.

## Local App Data

VoltRush AI may store learning progress locally on the user's device, such as XP, coins, streaks, mission progress, quiz results, achievements, selected learning path, and local settings. This information is used only to provide the in-app learning and game experience.

## Purchases

VoltRush AI includes StoreKit-ready subscriptions and in-app purchase placeholders. Any real purchase processing is handled by Apple through the App Store. The developer does not receive or store full payment card information.

## Notifications

VoltRush AI may ask permission to send notifications for reminders such as daily challenges. Notification permission can be changed at any time in iOS Settings.

## AI Mentor

The current AI Mentor feature uses local mock educational responses. No question or chat content is sent to an external AI service in this MVP. If a real AI service is added in the future, this policy and the App Store privacy information will be updated before release.

## Children's Privacy

VoltRush AI is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If future features introduce account creation, analytics, or online services, appropriate privacy disclosures and protections will be added.

## Changes

This privacy policy may be updated when VoltRush AI adds new features, services, analytics, purchases, or integrations.
